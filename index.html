About Us

At Baskien Burgers 2 we believe a great burger starts with great ingredients. Our team sources locally where possible and grills each patty to perfection.

Whether you're after a quick bite or a relaxed dinner, our cosy venue offers a welcoming atmosphere for families, friends and solo diners alike.
